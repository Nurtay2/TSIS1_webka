Burabay is the heart of the region is Lake Burabay, surrounded by dense pine forests and framed by the iconic Zhumbaktas Rock, which stands as a symbol of the area. Visitors to Burabay can enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, boating, and horseback riding, immersing themselves in the tranquility of the landscape.

Almaty, the largest city in Kazakhstan, is a vibrant metropolis that seamlessly blends modernity with a rich cultural heritage. Nestled at the foothills of the Trans-Ili Alatau mountains, Almaty offers a stunning backdrop of snow-capped peaks and lush landscapes. The city is known for its wide avenues, green parks, and a lively atmosphere. Almaty is a cultural hub, featuring a blend of traditional Kazakh influences and a cosmopolitan vibe.

Shymkent, the third-largest city in Kazakhstan, is a dynamic urban center with a rich history and a blend of cultural influences. Located in the southern part of the country, Shymkent serves as a gateway between Central Asia and the Middle East, contributing to its diverse and cosmopolitan atmosphere.
